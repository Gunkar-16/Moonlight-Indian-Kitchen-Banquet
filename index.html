About Us

Our Story

At Moonlight Indian Kitchen & Banquet, our journey is rooted in a passion for authentic Indian cuisine and heartfelt hospitality. Inspired by rich traditions and timeless recipes, we bring the true flavors of India to Edmonton in a space designed for comfort, warmth, and togetherness.

From carefully crafted dishes to our welcoming ambience, every detail is meant to make you feel at home. Whether you're here for a quiet dinner or a grand celebration, we are honored to be part of your special moments.
